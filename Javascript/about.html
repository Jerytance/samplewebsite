Owner: William Samhu-Kauyani

William Samhu-Kauyani is the proud owner of Southcape Butchery. He is also a contractor at National Foods Company, supplying them with all meat products excluding pork. William has a farm in Mazowe where he keeps dairy cows and Brahmas, which are meat cows.

William is a devout member of the Apostolic faith and does not sell pork products.

Location

Southcape Butchery is located at Juru Growth Point in Goromonzi, Mashonaland East Province, Zimbabwe. You can find us at the following coordinates: Juru Growth Point.

Our Specialties

At Southcape Butchery, we pride ourselves on providing the highest quality meat products. Our specialties include:

  • Fresh beef cuts
  • Organic dairy products
  • Specialty sausages
  • Custom meat orders for events and catering
